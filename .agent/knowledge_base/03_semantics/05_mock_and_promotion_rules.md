# Mock and Promotion Rules

## Purpose

Define when candidate concepts remain provisional and when they may become canon.

## Canonicality

Canonical for provisional-to-canonical transitions.

## Rules

- Mock concepts remain provisional until explicitly reviewed.
- A candidate idea requires destination classification and provenance before promotion.
- Promotion must be logged in `superset/promotion_log.json`.
- Persuasive wording alone never upgrades a concept to canon.
