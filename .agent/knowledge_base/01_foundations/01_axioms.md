# Axioms

## Purpose

Capture the foundational statements that constrain all later doctrine.

## Canonicality

Canonical for axioms only.

## Canonical axioms

### AX1: Primary Substrate

The primary substrate is a typed graph or hypergraph of objects and morphisms.

### AX2: Meaning Is Not Projection

Meaning is not identical to storage payload, metadata, embedding, or UI projection.

### AX3: Structural Truth Is Replayable

Structural truth is preserved through morphism composition and replayable change.

### AX4: Evaluation Does Not Redefine Ontology

Evaluation may materialize contingent views without altering foundational ontology.

### AX5: Governance Is Structural

Governance and capability are graph-structural, not external afterthoughts.
